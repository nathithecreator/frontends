Questions & Answers

What is UniNest Go?

UniNest Go is a mobile platform that helps students find safe, affordable, and verified housing near their universities.

Who can use UniNest Go?

UniNest Go is designed for both students looking for accommodation and landlords looking to rent to student tenants.

How does UniNest Go help students?

Students can search verified listings, sign leases digitally, request maintenance, match with roommates, and access local deals—all in one app.

Is UniNest Go safe to use?

Yes. Listings are verified, peer-reviewed, and part of a student-only community to reduce scams and build trust.

What are the benefits for landlords?

Landlords can reach verified student renters, boost listing visibility, manage applications, sign leases, and communicate—all through the app.
